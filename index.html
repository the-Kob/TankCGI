Controls

↑ : move the tank forward

↓ : move the tank backwards

w : move the pipe up

s : move the pipe down

a / d : rotate the turret

SPACE : shoot a projectile

1 / 2 / 3 / 4 : change your view in the scene

W : toggle wireframe on

S : toggle wireframe off

+ : zoom in

- : zoom out

c : show and hide the controls list
